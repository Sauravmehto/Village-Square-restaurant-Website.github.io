Catering
Village Square Restaurant provides both on-site and off-site catering for a variety of events to include:

  • Rehearsal Dinners
  • Weddings
  • Anniversary Parties
  • Showers
  • Family Functions
  • Board Meetings
  • Corporate Meetings
  • Pharmaceutical Presentations

We can accommodate a variety of party sizes or you may book the entire restaurant.

We customize all menus to suit your group’s needs. All vegetarian, vegan or any other dietary requests will be honored. If site permits, plated dinners can be arranged. Additional fees may apply for rentals of equipment and supplies. Village Square can provide serving staff and bartenders if the customer requires.

 

Please contact us for menu and pricing. Thank you.
